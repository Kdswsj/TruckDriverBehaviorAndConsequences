Truck Driver Driving Behavior and Consequences

Kim D Sorensen


Galvanize Capstone Project, January 2018


This project is carried out in collaboration with Predictive Fleet Technologies, Midland, MI


The overall objective is to classify truck driver behaviors into groups and identify relative costs pr mile for each group


The purpose is to improve the hiring process and to recommend training/termination decisions to improve safety and profitability
